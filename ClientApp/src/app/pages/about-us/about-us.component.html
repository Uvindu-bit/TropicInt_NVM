About us

WELCOME TO TROPIC INTERNATIONAL PVT LTD

We are a family own import company based in London, UK have been operating in the importation of fresh fruits, vegetables, and cut foliage to Uk over the last 12 years. As we have our strong family connections with Sri Lanka, we have been importing all the items from Sri Lanka through our own export processing center based in the country. We pride our selves being able to employ the vast majority of the workforce from our locality and being able to source from all parts of the country for our fresh produce. As we have now started importing a range of fruits and vegetables from India we are now able to offer an even wider range of a selection of commodities to our valued customers.

DISTRIBUTION AND TRANSPORTAION.

As consignments arrive throughout the week, London and out of London distribution can be arranged for any customer within the mainland to match their individual needs.
